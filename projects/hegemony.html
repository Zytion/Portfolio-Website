Hegemony

Overview

Hegemony is a three to six player semi-cooperative game where each of you play as a noble of one of the three Ethshars. This game will be a unique experience no matter how many times you play, with unique goals and events that can change the entire flow of the game. Will you be a generous and fair noble or will you be corrupt and betray your people? It's up to you to find out in Hegemony.

My Role

  • Creating and assigning tasks for each sprint
  • Designing and testing card abilities
  • In charge of game balancing
  • Putting together presentation material
