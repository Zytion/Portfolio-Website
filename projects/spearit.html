Spearit

Overview

The game is a 2D parallax side-scrolling game where you have a spear that you can throw which when thrown you can teleport to it. You are an amorphous figure with a cowboy hat sent by higher powers to fight against a cult that worships the technologies of the past. The starting area will be the ruins of a city, reclaimed by the forest, with bits and pieces of modern or futuristic technology in disarray.

My Role

I was the project manager and main game designer for this project. This work involved many tasks such as:

  • Defining project MVP
  • Creating and assigning tasks for each sprint (Trello)
  • Designing game elements
  • Working with team members to match product to design goals
  • Putting together presentation material
  • Assembling team products into single deliverable
