Witch Way Out

Overview

You play as Karanissa the Witch, from the Legends of Ethshar book series, who is trapped in the pocket dimension. The way we will represent this is through the art and color scheme of the levels. We are also representing this through the limited amount of spells that Karanissa can cast each level as witches are held back by their life-force. Witch Way Out is a casual puzzle platformer where you use your magical powers to traverse the level and clear obstacles to reach the finish. These powers could involve various things such as freezing spells, speed manipulation, time dilation, air jumps, and more. You will have a limited amount of uses of these abilities to complete the levels.

My Role

I was the Project Manager and game designer for this project. This work involved many tasks such as:

  • Creating an MVP
  • Defining and assigning tasks each week to team
  • Designing UI/UX for casting spells
  • Assembling team products into final game
  • Putting together presentation material
  • Presented final project
